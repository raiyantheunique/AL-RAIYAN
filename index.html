Photography Portfolio

A selection of captured moments from my travels.

Photography, for me, is the art of **seeing beyond the obvious**. It starts not with the camera, but with **light**‚Äîunderstanding how it falls, where it creates drama, and how it shapes the subject. The foundation of a great image lies in **composition**; using principles like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and negative space to guide the viewer's eye and create visual balance.
